Workplace Activity & Health Study
You are about to begin the Baseline Survey for a longitudinal workplace health research study. This study tracks how physical activity affects employee well-being, energy levels, and retention over time.
Your responses are used solely for academic research and will contribute to a published paper on workplace health.
Data is stored securely. Individual responses will be anonymized in any published findings.
Participation is voluntary. You may stop at any time, though completing all surveys helps ensure accurate results.
After the baseline, you will receive Monthly, Quarterly, and Annual follow-up surveys as they unlock.
By clicking "I Understand & Agree", you confirm you are 18+ and voluntarily consent to participate in this research study.
